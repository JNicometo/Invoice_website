Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

Our Privacy Commitment

At InvoicePro, we take your privacy seriously. Unlike cloud-based solutions, our desktop application stores all your data locally on your computer. We don't have access to your invoices, clients, or financial information.

Data Collection

Application Data: All invoices, client information, and business data you create in InvoicePro is stored locally on your computer. We do not collect, store, or transmit this data to our servers.

Purchase Information: When you purchase InvoicePro, our payment processor collects your name, email address, and payment information. We receive your name and email address to deliver your license key.

Support Communications: If you contact our support team, we may collect your email address and any information you choose to share to help resolve your issue.

How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver your software license and updates
  • To provide customer support
  • To send important product announcements (you can opt out anytime)
  • To improve our product based on feedback

Data Security

Your application data is stored locally on your device and is only as secure as your device itself. We recommend:

  • Using strong password protection on your computer
  • Regularly backing up your data
  • Keeping your operating system and security software up to date

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services for payment processing. These services have their own privacy policies and handle payment information securely. We never see or store your full credit card information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your application data at any time (it's already on your computer!)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through the application.
